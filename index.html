What BEAM-AI produces

  • Input Upload a PDB or AlphaFold structure—BEAM-AI ingests the raw coordinates.
  • BEAM-AI does Reads the surface, predicts high-likelihood pockets, and designs candidate binders tailored to those regions.
  • Output A ranked shortlist with BEAM-Scores so teams immediately focus on the tightest predicted binders.

Why It Matters
Typical blind screens: 1–5% hits.

Trial-and-error wastes cycles in the lab with tiny hit-rates.

BEAM-AI top picks: ~30% hits.

Focus resources on the most promising binders first.

From idea to validation

1 · Hypothesis Identify a tumor-relevant surface to target.
2 · BEAM-AI Rank & design binders in weeks, not years.
3 · Lab Confirm top designs and move to in-vitro tests.

What We’re Building Next

This is the roadmap: a future BEAM-AI workflow that turns today’s prototype into an end-to-end engine for structure-guided discovery.

1. Surface Intelligence

Scale up physics-informed surface analysis so every new protein upload comes with an instant map of high-probability binding zones.

2. Generative Binder Design

Layer on multi-modal generative models that tailor antibodies, peptides, or small molecules to those zones—delivering a shortlist instead of a haystack.

3. Learn & Loop

Close the loop: feed partner lab results back into BEAM-AI so scoring tightens and the recommendations get sharper with every cycle.

When paired with Oncera’s Tumor Avatars, this roadmap aims to validate designs on patient-relevant models before costly trials—accelerating the path from insight to clinic.
